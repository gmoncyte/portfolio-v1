Personal mark

Project was created during lectures

This project started in 2020 and was created during lectures in Kaunas University of Applied Sciences Academy of Arts. After days of conceptualizing and sketching numerous ideas, I came up with a simple, strong mark that encapsulated my values. The inspiration was from my first name and surname initials - G and M. The main idea was to make a symbol that depicts my own personality, so I chose neat, strict and clear design of my own mark. Selected colors are white and black, because in that kind of color combination reveals the characteristics of a personal mark in it's best.
