Flat objects

Flat objects

Free time projects

I have had a graphics tablet for a long time, but I never did it until I wanted to start illustrating from photos. I ran this personal project for two weeks. I was looking, as the most primitive planes, to subtly but neatly depict the person in the photo and adjusted certain details to make the drawing more interesting. I was intensely looking for details and eye-catching color. These illustrations are fit perfectly for decorating home walls or even reflecting on textiles.
