academic and creative drawings

Free time and lecture works

I have a great passion for academic drawing. The quality of the drawings tells perfectly what a creator I am. In my academic drawing, I focus all my attention on the details: small fragments, shadows, tones, and halftones, falling light. I like to analyze deeper the things I portray. I try to follow logic and I think I have a great command of drawing. At times i like to compose something creative in my strict drawing, to get out of the framework of academic drawing. I drew most of the presented drawings on my own. Drawings are drawn with a pencil on toned paper in A2 format. Drawings and sketches were made in 2016-2021.
