Tattoo works

Free time projects

Through these two projects, I sought to improve my drawing skills and unleash my imagination. The characters of the works are of a mystical nature. These sketches have been performed very carefully, and they are rich in various details and fragments. The aim of the dynamic plot is to draw the viewer's attention, to encourage them to delve into the work, to examine it more closely. The main focus is on the plasticity of the lines, the search for size variations. The purpose of these sketches is for tattoos, but it would also be perfect for the interior on a wall or on a textile fabric. The sketches were made in 2021.
