Mysterious forest

Papercup project "Mysterious forest"

Project was created during lectures

This project started in 2019 and was created during lectures in Kaunas University of Applied Sciences Academy of Arts. After several weeks of analyzing and sketching paper cup illustration designs I created strong, mystical design for paper cup. This design have a lot of plastic shapes and lines, cold gradient colors and noisy texture. The cup design depicts a forest where coffee beans are pouring. This graphic solutionand its colors are intendedto express the taste of coffee magic, mystery.The cup design helps to enjoy the magic palette of flavors.
